Privacy Policy

This service stores account details, course profile data, and conversation history needed to operate the platform.

What we store

  • Account email, hashed password, role, and organization details
  • Course profile data such as region, surfaces, and management notes
  • Conversation history, feedback, and admin moderation records

How data is used

  • To personalize responses and maintain course-specific context
  • To improve answer quality, moderation, and safety review
  • To secure admin access and account ownership

Operational note

This deployment currently uses file-backed account storage and local application databases. Production deployments should use secure backups, access controls, and infrastructure monitoring appropriate to customer requirements.
